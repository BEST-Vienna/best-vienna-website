About us

Our Local Group

84 Local BEST Groups in 30 countries create a powerful, young, dynamic and innovative student network. In Vienna, more than 20 members work every day to provide activities, career days, competitions and academic courses for TU Wien students.

Our Mission

What We Stand For

BEST is a constantly growing non-profit and non-political organisation. Since 1989 we provide communication, co-operation and exchange possibilities for students all over Europe.

Our Community

The BEST Triangle

Our priority is to offer high-quality services and build links between the 3 members of the BEST triangle: Students, Partners and Universities. Learn more at best.eu.org.
